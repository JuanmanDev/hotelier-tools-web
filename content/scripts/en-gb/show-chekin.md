## What does this script do?

Connects check-in data directly into the reservation details.
See at a glance if guests have checked in while checking which room is assigned, as well as their registration data and pending payment amount.

**Features:**
- Streamlines check-in data review
- Avoids switching between tabs or apps
- Reduces guest wait time upon arrival
- Directly copies the ID from Chekin to use in the invoice
- Get more guest phone numbers to contact them in case of any problem

Automatically retrieves and displays guest registration data from Chekin.com, including guest names, phone numbers, and document IDs, with options to share registration forms via email or WhatsApp.
