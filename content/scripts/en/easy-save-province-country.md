## What does it do?

This script streamlines the process of filling out guest details by providing an autocomplete list for Spanish provinces and automatically filling the related country field.

**Features:**
- Shows a list of Spanish provinces to select when focusing on the province field.
- Automatically sets the country to "Spain" when a province is selected.
- Sets an underscore `_` for empty name and surname fields, meeting the requirements for the upcoming INE integration.
